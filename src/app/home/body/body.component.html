About TopRate Transfer

Top Rate Transfer is a money remittance service company. Our aim is to provide unbeatable exchange rates to our customer and focus on delivering fast, affordable and reliable money remittance service from Australia to countries around the world. With beneficiary satisfaction. Our business model is centred on foreign exchange and money transfer service, founded on the principles of speed, security, satisfaction, fairness, transparency, efficiency and affordability. Our platform is secured with latest technology and security at a bank security level and integrated to perform transactions on computers, tablets and smart phones. Top Rate Transfer is licensed under AUSTRAC and compliance is very important to us to ensure transparency in all we do. We are fully committed to compliance with the Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorism Financing Act 2006 and Regulations.

How to Send Money with TopRate Transfer

person_add Sign up for free

Download our Mobile App from Apple App Store or Google Play Store. There is also a link on our website to download our mobile app.

touch_app Activate Your Account

Upon signing up, an activation code will be sent to your email address.

account_box Sign In

After the activation of your account, you can sign in with your email and password credentials.

fingerprint Verify your Identity

Due to AUSTRAC regulations, sender ID must be verified before we can process the Transaction.

swap_horiz Initiate Transaction

Enter the total amount of money you are sending, and it will automatically convert it to the total amount to receive. You can make payment either by OSKO(Pay ID) or Poli Payment.

sentiment_satisfied_alt Complete Transfer

Upon ID verification and payment confirmation, Fund will be deposited to the receiver nominated account.
